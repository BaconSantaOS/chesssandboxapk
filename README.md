# Chess Sandbox - Android App

Break the rules of chess in this innovative game where anything is possible. Chess Sandbox lets you play chess your way with complete freedom to experiment, learn, and create.

Visit the website: https://chesssandbox.com/

## About Chess Sandbox

Chess Sandbox is a unique chess experience that removes traditional restrictions and lets you play freely. Whether you want to test unconventional strategies, practice specific positions, or just have fun breaking all the rules - Chess Sandbox is your playground.

Available on both PC (web) and Android mobile devices.

## Download Source Code

Go to: https://github.com/BaconSantaOS/chesssandboxapk

Click the green “Code” button and select “Download ZIP”

Extract the folder and you’ll have the APK file.

That’s it. Install it on your Android phone and play.

## Installation

1. Open the APK file on your Android phone
2. Click “Install”
3. Open Chess Sandbox and start playing

## Project Files

- app-release.apk - The app file (install this on your phone)

## How to Play

1. Launch the app
1. Select Online or Offline mode
1. Break the chess rules however you want
1. Move pieces freely without restrictions
1. Save or share your games
